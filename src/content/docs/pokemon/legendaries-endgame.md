Kayri Ranch has **two parallel endgame arcs** — pick either, or both.

## Arc A — Legendary Monument (Rayquaza)

**Legendary Monuments** are structures that, once found and activated with the right ritual, summon a legendary Pokémon. The canonical endgame legendary is **Rayquaza**, summoned via the sky-tower monument.

The ritual typically involves:

- Finding the monument structure (they're rare)
- Collecting specific items or defeating specific Pokémon
- Activating the monument

Once summoned, the legendary encounter is **high-level and high-reward** — successful capture or defeat unlocks significant rewards.

Specific steps are spoiler-sensitive; ask Fett or a veteran player when you're ready.

## Arc B — Vanilla MC bosses

The **Ender Dragon** is still active — travel to the End, defeat it as in vanilla MC. Respawn mechanics work normally.

The **Wither** can still be summoned (wither skeleton skulls + soul sand), but since no wither skeletons spawn naturally, skulls come from a rare **Marowak** drop. Gathering 3 is a project.

Both bosses are in the pack; neither is required for pack progression, but both count as meaningful challenges.

## Tier context

- Mid-game: completing most gyms, building a strong team, setting up automation
- Late-game: clearing raid dens, finishing regional Pokémon coverage, scaling gear
- Endgame: legendary monument or vanilla boss run

There's **no hard gate** — the server is permanent (no wipes), so go at your pace.
