## Trigger

Fire-type Pokémon

## What it does

Generates a lava block in the scan zone.

## Cadence

Every **90 seconds**

## Setup requirements

Empty air slot in scan zone

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Vulpix / Ninetales
- Ponyta / Rapidash
- Cyndaquil / Quilava / Typhlosion
- Any Fire-type

## Tips

- **Build a lava-proof containment** — bucket it out or pipe to a collector
- Pair with a furnace stack that accepts lava buckets as fuel
