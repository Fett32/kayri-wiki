Welcome to **Kayri Ranch** — a small, friendly modded Cobblemon server. The pack is a Fabric 1.21.1 build with 200+ mods, a custom Pokémon-drops ecosystem, 1500+ trainers, and a few hundred waystones already placed across the world. Bring patience on your first launch (mods download) and a willingness to poke around.

## The big things to know

- **All vanilla mobs are gone** — overworld, Nether, and End. Wild Pokémon fill the ecosystem everywhere. Ender Dragon stays as the End boss; Wither can still be summoned. Villagers, iron golems, etc. are kept.
- **Wild Pokémon can get aggressive** — if one charges you, hit **G** to start a battle.
- **Yellow-roofed buildings are Gyms** — each has a healing machine and a Gym Leader to challenge.
- **Pokémon Centers** (scattered villages) also heal your team and have traders.
- **Pokémon can automate your base.** Certain Pokémon can man workstations for you — furnaces, farms, apricorn trees, crafting, honeycomb, etc. Look into [Pasture Automation](/base/pasture-automation/) once you're settled.
- **Temperature matters.** Desert = overheat, snowy = frostbite. Dress accordingly, bring water in hot biomes.
- **No wipes.** The world is permanent — your builds, progress, and base stay.
