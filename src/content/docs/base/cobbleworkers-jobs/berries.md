## Trigger

Grass-type Pokémon

## What it does

Harvests mature Cobblemon berries from berry trees. Berry trees regrow on their own.

## Cadence

Passive — as berries ripen

## Setup requirements

Berry trees in scan zone

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Same as Crop Harvest — any Grass-type works

## Tips

- Berries matter for hold items (Sitrus, Leppa, etc.) — worth automating early
- Seed a chest with one of each berry type to route them to separate slots
