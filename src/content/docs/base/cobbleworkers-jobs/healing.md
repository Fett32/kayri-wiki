## Trigger

Chansey / Blissey / Happiny **OR** any Pokémon that knows a listed healing move

## What it does

Grants **Regen I (20s)** to nearby players.

## Cadence

Triggered — when a player is in range and under a threshold

## Setup requirements

Nothing — just the healer Pokémon in the pasture

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Chansey / Blissey / Happiny
- Any mon knowing: **wish, softboiled, moonlight, recover, roost, healbell, aromatherapy, synthesis, rest, lifedew**

## Tips

- Chansey line triggers healing regardless of moveset
- Other species need one of the listed moves taught/natural
- Great at crafting stations and AFK spots
- Config can boost: `regenAmplifier: 1` → Regen II; `regenDurationSeconds: 40` → doubled duration
