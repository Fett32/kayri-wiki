## Trigger

Water-type Pokémon

## What it does

Waters nearby farmland — keeps it hydrated without a water block adjacent.

## Cadence

Passive — continuous while in pasture

## Setup requirements

Farmland in scan zone

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Squirtle / Psyduck / Mudkip / Totodile / Oshawott / Froakie / Piplup / Marill / Buizel / Sobble / Quaxly lines

## Tips

- **Fish-shaped Water-types don't work** — Tentacool, Magikarp, Wailmer, Feebas, Remoraid, Goldeen can't leave water. Use land-capable water mons.
- Pairs perfectly with a Grass-type pasture doing Crop Harvest
