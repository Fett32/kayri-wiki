## Trigger

Fairy-type Pokémon

## What it does

Harvests mature Cobblemon mints.

## Cadence

Passive — as mints ripen

## Setup requirements

Cobblemon mint plants in scan zone

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Clefairy line
- Togepi → Togetic → Togekiss
- Snubbull → Granbull
- Any Fairy-type

## Tips

- Mints let you re-roll a Pokémon's nature — useful endgame
- Not high-volume but worth automating once you have Fairy-types
