## Trigger

Dragon-type Pokémon

## What it does

Adds 5 fuel units to nearby brewing stands.

## Cadence

Every **80 seconds**

## Setup requirements

Brewing stand in scan zone

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Dratini → Dragonair → Dragonite
- Bagon → Shelgon → Salamence
- Deino line
- Any Dragon-type

## Tips

- Dragon-types are rare early but this is a permanent potion-brewing setup
- Pair with Chansey line for AFK potion-drinking + regen
