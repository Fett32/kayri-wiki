## Trigger

Fire-type Pokémon

## What it does

Generates furnace fuel — 80s burn time per unit.

## Cadence

Every **80 seconds**

## Setup requirements

Any furnace in scan zone (vanilla, smoker, blast furnace)

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Any Fire-type

## Tips

- Burn time matches vanilla coal (80s) — effectively an unlimited smelter
- Stack multiple furnaces around one Fire pasture for bulk smelting
