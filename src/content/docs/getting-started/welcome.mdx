Welcome to **Kayri Ranch** — a small, friendly modded Cobblemon server. Fabric 1.21.1, 200+ mods, a custom Pokémon-drops ecosystem, 1500+ trainers, and a few hundred waystones scattered across the world. Bring patience for your first launch (mods download), and a willingness to poke around.

<Aside type="tip" title="Good luck, trainer">
If you're reading this, you're probably about to join for the first time. Don't stress the details — just explore, catch some Pokémon, die a few times, rebuild better. The world is permanent.
</Aside>

## The big things to know

<CardGrid>
	<Card title="No vanilla hostile mobs" icon="seti:lock">
		Overworld, Nether, and End — all cleared. Wild Pokémon fill the ecosystem. Ender Dragon stays as the End boss; Wither can still be summoned. Villagers and iron golems are kept.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Wild Pokémon can attack" icon="warning">
		If one charges you, hit **G** to start a battle. Fight or Flight is active.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Yellow-roofed = Gyms" icon="star">
		Each has a Healing Machine and a Gym Leader to challenge. Beating them is progression.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Pokémon Centers heal + trade" icon="heart">
		Scattered across villages. Also have merchants who take Pokédollars.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Pokémon can automate your base" icon="setting">
		Certain types do jobs — farms, furnaces, fishing, crafting. See [Pasture Automation](/base/pasture-automation/) once you're settled.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Temperature matters" icon="sun">
		Desert = overheat. Snowy = frostbite. Dress accordingly, carry water in hot biomes. See [Temperature Survival](/base/temperature-survival/).
	</Card>
	<Card title="The world is permanent" icon="approve-check-circle">
		No wipes. Your builds, your progress, and your base stay. Build like you mean it.
	</Card>
</CardGrid>
