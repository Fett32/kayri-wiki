## Trigger

Psychic-type Pokémon

## What it does

Picks up dropped items on the ground and deposits them to nearby containers.

## Cadence

Passive — as items appear

## Setup requirements

Items on the ground in scan zone

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Abra / Kadabra / Alakazam
- Ralts → Kirlia → Gardevoir / Gallade
- Munna → Musharna
- Any Psychic-type

## Tips

- Great for mob farms / drop zones where items pile up
- Seeds chest-routing same as other jobs — seed destination chests
