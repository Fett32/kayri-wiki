## Trigger

Ice-type Pokémon

## What it does

Generates snow layers on suitable surfaces.

## Cadence

Every **90 seconds**

## Setup requirements

Open snow-layer spot in scan zone (grass/dirt/etc., exposed to sky)

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Seel / Dewgong
- Swinub line (Piloswine / Mamoswine)
- Any Ice-type

## Tips

- Accumulates quickly — set up a scoop mechanism
- Useful for ice farms and decorative winter zones
