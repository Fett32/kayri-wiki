## Trigger

Water-type Pokémon

## What it does

Puts out nearby fires automatically.

## Cadence

Passive — fires are extinguished when detected

## Setup requirements

Nothing special — fires appearing in scan zone

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Any land-capable Water-type

## Tips

- Pair with a Fire-type pasture generating lava for safety
- Useful for wooden builds if you have Fire-types around
