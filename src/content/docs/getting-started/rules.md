Kept short on purpose.

## The rules

- **Don't grief.** Don't break, burn, flood, or otherwise damage other players' builds, farms, or paths.
- **Don't be a jerk.** Disagreements happen — work them out without harassment or sustained rudeness.
- **Have fun. Or don't. But don't ruin others' fun.** If what you're doing is making someone else's time worse, stop.
- **Thanks for reading this far.**

## What counts as griefing

A few specifics that have come up or might:

- Opening other players' chests/storage without explicit permission
- Taking Pokémon from other players' pastures
- Intentionally spawning hostile Pokémon at someone's base
- Leaving open holes, lava, or traps near active build areas

When in doubt: ask first, apologize fast.

## Whitelist, PvP, and claims

- **Whitelist-only.** Players are invited by trusted existing players. Ask Fett or a server officer.
- **No PvP combat intended.** Fight or Flight lets wild Pokémon attack you, but no player-vs-player expectation.
- **No claim system.** Bases are respected socially, not enforced by mod. See "What counts as griefing" above.

## Reporting problems

Message Fett or an officer on Discord with what happened, when, and ideally a screenshot or coords. Don't try to resolve escalating situations yourself.
