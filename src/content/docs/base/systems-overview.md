Once you've got the basics down, here's what else the pack has. You don't need to engage with all of it — just know what's there.

## Advanced Pokémon

- **Mega Evolution, Z-Moves, Tera, Dynamax, Ultra Burst, Primal, Fusions** — all active via *Mega Showdown* + *Navas ZA Megas*. See [Content Overview](/pokemon/content-overview/).
- **Alphas** — Legends-Arceus-style giant, stronger wild Pokémon.
- **Variants** — tons of fan/anime/regional forms (Crystal Onix, Mega Gardevoir, etc.) via *AllTheMons* + *AllTheMegas*.
- **Breeding** via *Cobbreeding* — egg moves, natures, hereditary stats.

## Things to fight

- **1500+ Trainers** scattered in the world (*Radical Cobblemon Trainers*) — defeat them for Pokédollars + rewards.
- **Gyms & Gym Leaders** at Radical Gyms structures — see [Gyms](/pokemon/gyms/).
- **Raid Dens** — boss-level Pokémon with rare drops — see [Raids](/pokemon/raids/).
- **Legendary Monuments** — rituals to summon legendary Pokémon. **Rayquaza = intended endgame.**
- **Ender Dragon** — still exists as a parallel endgame target.

## Structures & dungeons

- **Terralith + Tectonic** — way more varied biomes than vanilla.
- **YUNG's Better X** — better Strongholds, Mineshafts, Nether Fortresses, Ocean Monuments, Witch Huts, etc.
- **Dungeons Arise** — large standalone dungeon structures.
- **Incendium** (Nether overhaul), **Enderscape** (End overhaul).
- Ancient Cities, Grand Capitals, extra villages, desert mansions, ice castles, more.

## Combat & gear

- **Better Combat** — weapon combos, directional attacks.
- **Simply Swords** — new weapon types.
- **Immersive Armors** — more armor variety.
- **Accessories** — slots for belt, gloves, ring, etc. Open with **H**.
- **Quiver** (**V**) — Supplementaries bow quiver.

## Life / base-building

- **Traveler's Backpack** (**J**) — wearable storage.
- **Farmer's Delight + CobbleDelight** — cooking system with real recipes.
- **Cobbleworkers** — Pokémon automate farms/crafts around your base. See [Pasture Automation](/base/pasture-automation/).
- **Chipped** — decorative variants for every vanilla block family. See [Decorating with Chipped](/base/chipped/).
- **Waystones** — shared fast-travel network. See [Waystones](/base/waystones/).

## Nice-to-have QoL

- **Jade** — hover over anything for info (durability, contents, etc.).
- **Knowlogy** — hover over Pokémon for type / ability / stats.
- **Cobblepedia** — in-game Pokémon reference.
- **Pokenav** — GPS / tracking for Pokémon.
- **Full Bright** on **B** — toggle cave brightness.

## Power-user tools (optional)

**Litematica**, **MiniHUD**, and **Tweakeroo** are installed (schematic building, debug overlay, gameplay tweaks). Hotkeys moved off conflicts — see [Keybinds](/getting-started/keybinds/). Most players won't touch these.
