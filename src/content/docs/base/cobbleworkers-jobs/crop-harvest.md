## Trigger

Grass-type Pokémon

## What it does

Harvests wheat, carrots, potatoes, and beetroot. **Automatically replants.**

## Cadence

Passive — as crops mature

## Setup requirements

Mature crops in scan zone (farmland + water nearby if no Water-type pasture)

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Oddish / Gloom / Vileplume line
- Bellsprout / Weepinbell / Victreebel line
- Bulbasaur → Venusaur
- Any Grass starter

## Tips

- Pair with a Water-type pasture for automatic irrigation
- Seed a chest with one wheat/carrot/etc. so harvested crops route there automatically
