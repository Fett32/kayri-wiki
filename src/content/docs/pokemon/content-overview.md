Kayri Ranch runs Cobblemon 1.7.3 plus AllTheMons, AllTheMegas, Radical Gyms, Cobblemon Trainers, and more. This page is a **spoiler-light** tour of what *kinds* of Pokémon and mechanics exist — no species lists, no boss details, no location spoilers.

## Battle mechanics in play

| Mechanic | Era | Enabler |
|---|---|---|
| **Mega Evolution** | Gen 6 | Mega Stones (held) |
| **Z-Moves** | Gen 7 | Z-Crystals |
| **Ultra Burst** | Gen 7 | Necrozma-specific |
| **Dynamax / Gigantamax** | Gen 8 | Dynamax Band |
| **Tera** | Gen 9 | Tera Orb |
| **Primal Reversion** | Gen 6 | Kyogre / Groudon |
| **Fusions** | Gen 5/7 | Kyurem & Necrozma |
| **Paradox forms** | Gen 9 | Dual / Paradox variants where applicable |

## Variants you'll run into

- **Regional variants** — Alolan, Galarian, Hisuian, Paldean, and plenty of fan regional variants.
- **Anime / manga / side-game canon forms** — Crystal Onix, Shadow Pokémon, movie-exclusive forms, manga-lore Pokémon (via **AllTheMons**).
- **Non-canon Megas** — many Pokémon that never got Megas officially have fan-made Megas (via **AllTheMegas**).
- **Alphas** — Legends-Arceus-style giant, stronger wild Pokémon. Rare, dangerous, usually high-level.
- **Shiny variants** — standard rate.

## What's in the world

- **Wild spawns** — Pokémon spawn in biome-appropriate habitats. Density is a bit higher than Cobblemon default. See [Spawn Locations](/pokemon/spawn-locations/) for per-biome details.
- **Trainers** — 1500+ NPC trainers scattered across the world, organized in tiers.
- **Gyms** — Gym Leaders with themed teams; beating them is a progression goal. See [Gyms](/pokemon/gyms/).
- **Raid Dens** — boss-level Pokémon encounters with rare loot. See [Raids](/pokemon/raids/).
- **Legendary Monuments** — summoning rituals for legendary Pokémon. See [Legendaries & Endgame](/pokemon/legendaries-endgame/).
- **A specific legendary is the intended endgame boss.** Whether you go for that or the vanilla Ender Dragon is your call — both exist.

## Nether Pokémon (yes, visit)

All vanilla hostile mobs are removed from the Nether — but Pokémon still spawn. Worth visiting for:

- **Falinks** *(Nether-exclusive)*
- Charcadet → Armarouge / Ceruledge
- Honedge → Doublade → Aegislash
- Darumaka → Darmanitan
- Houndour → Houndoom
- Slugma → Magcargo
- Gastly → Haunter → Gengar
- Zorua → Zoroark

## End Pokémon (also visit)

Vanilla mobs gone except the **Ender Dragon** (still End boss). Pokémon that spawn in End biomes:

- **Beldum → Metang → Metagross** *(End-exclusive — pseudo-legendary line)*
- Gothita → Gothorita → Gothitelle
- Solosis → Duosion → Reuniclus
- Elgyem → Beheeyem
- Inkay → Malamar
- Unown (all letters)
- Sigilyph

*Installed variant datapacks may add more species to both dimensions.*

## What's *not* here

- **No Pokédex "catch 'em all" progression arc.** You explore and catch what you find.
- **No Pokémon League bracket.** Gyms are standalone structures, not a rigid order.
- **No PvE story mode.** The world is the story.

## Tools to look things up in-game

- **Cobblepedia** — in-game Pokémon reference (spawn biomes, evolutions, moves).
- **Knowlogy** — hover any wild Pokémon for its stats / type / ability.
- **Pokenav** — GPS-style Pokémon tracker.
