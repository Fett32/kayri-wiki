## Trigger

Water-type Pokémon

## What it does

Generates a water block in the scan zone. Useful for setting up farms without an existing water source.

## Cadence

Every **90 seconds**

## Setup requirements

Empty air slot in scan zone where water can be placed

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Any land-capable Water-type (see Irrigation for list)

## Tips

- Great for bases far from natural water
- One water block irrigates crops in a 4-block radius — place smartly
