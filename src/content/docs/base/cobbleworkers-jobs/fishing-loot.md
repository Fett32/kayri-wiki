## Trigger

Water-type Pokémon (land-capable)

## What it does

Generates fishing loot from the fishing loot table — fish, treasure, and junk (1% treasure chance).

## Cadence

Every **60 seconds**

## Setup requirements

Water block in scan zone

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Any land-capable Water-type

## Tips

- Treasure includes enchanted books and rare items — passive enchanting farm
- Rolls come from vanilla fishing table, same as manual fishing
