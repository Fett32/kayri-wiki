## Trigger

Combee / Vespiquen line

## What it does

Generates honey and can harvest beehives.

## Cadence

Every **120 seconds**

## Setup requirements

Can also harvest adjacent beehives if `combeeLineCollectsHoney: true` (it is, by default)

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Combee
- Vespiquen

## Tips

- Only the Combee/Vespiquen line — type-based rule doesn't apply here
- Honey is used in cooking recipes and some Cobblemon crafting
