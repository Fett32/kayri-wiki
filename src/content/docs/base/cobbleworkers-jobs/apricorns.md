## Trigger

Bug-type Pokémon

## What it does

Harvests colored apricorns from mature apricorn trees.

## Cadence

Passive — as apricorns mature

## Setup requirements

Apricorn trees in scan zone

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Caterpie line / Weedle line
- Venonat → Venomoth
- Scyther (before evo)
- Any Bug-type

## Tips

- Apricorns are crafted into Poké Balls — the early-to-mid-game bottleneck this solves
- Works across all colors; plant a grove of mixed apricorn trees under one pasture
