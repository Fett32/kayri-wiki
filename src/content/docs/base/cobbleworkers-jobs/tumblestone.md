## Trigger

Steel-type Pokémon

## What it does

Harvests Cobblemon tumblestone blocks. **Automatically replants.**

## Cadence

Passive — as tumblestone matures

## Setup requirements

Tumblestone patches in scan zone (placed by you)

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Magnemite line
- Aron → Lairon → Aggron
- Beldum → Metang → Metagross
- Any Steel-type

## Tips

- Tumblestone is a Cobblemon-specific crafting resource
- Replanting means one patch farms itself indefinitely
