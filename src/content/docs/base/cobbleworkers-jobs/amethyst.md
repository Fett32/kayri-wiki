## Trigger

Rock-type Pokémon

## What it does

Harvests mature amethyst clusters from amethyst geodes.

## Cadence

Passive — as clusters mature

## Setup requirements

Amethyst geode with mature clusters in scan zone

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Geodude line
- Rhyhorn / Rhydon / Rhyperior
- Onix / Steelix
- Any Rock-type

## Tips

- Amethyst is a key material for some decor + Cobblemon recipes
- Works best on exposed geode interiors; shove a pasture right into the hollow
