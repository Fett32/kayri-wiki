## What a Raid Den is

A **Raid Den** is a structure (usually a glowing pillar or dome) that spawns a **Max Raid-style boss Pokémon** when activated. Think Sword/Shield max raids — a single strong Pokémon you fight, often with the Dynamax mechanic active.

## How to start a raid

- Find a Raid Den structure in the world (Pokenav helps)
- Activate it to spawn the boss
- Battle solo or with server players in cooperative mode (if supported)
- **Loot drops on completion** — rare items, TMs, evolution stones, sometimes held items

## Difficulty tiers

Raids scale by tier. Tier is usually visible on the den itself:

- **1★ – 2★** — moderate, solo-able with an in-level team
- **3★ – 4★** — hard, ideally co-op or a well-prepared solo team
- **5★** — brutal, prepare accordingly; legendary-tier Pokémon

## Strategy tips

- **Status moves help.** Raid bosses can have Dynamax shields — status hits (Toxic, Paralyze, Sleep) bypass physical bulk.
- **Bring revives.** Faints are expected in higher-tier raids.
- **Check type matchups before engaging** — a mismatched team will burn through revives.
- **Cooperate if possible.** Multiple players in one raid is much safer.
