## Trigger

Aquatic Pokémon

## What it does

Dives for shipwreck loot — gilded chest drops.

## Cadence

Every **210 seconds**

## Setup requirements

Water block in scan zone (for the dive animation)

## Good Pokémon for this job

- Lapras
- Gyarados
- Sharpedo
- Kyogre (if you've got one)
- Aquatic-tagged mons

## Tips

- Longest cooldown of any job — slow but valuable
- Loot table: `cobblemon:shipwreck_coves/gilded_chests/lesser_treasure`
- Pair with a Water-gen pasture if you don't have natural water
