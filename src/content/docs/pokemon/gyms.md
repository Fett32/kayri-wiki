## What to look for

- **Yellow-roofed buildings** in villages or standalone in the world. That's the signal.
- Each gym has:
  - A **Healing Machine** near the entrance (use before challenging)
  - A **Gym Leader** with a themed team (ice, fire, fighting, etc.)
  - **Rematches** often available after a cooldown

## How challenges work

- Walk up to the Gym Leader to initiate.
- Your whole party is eligible — it's a full Pokémon battle.
- Defeat their team to win; you get **rewards** (TMs, items, progression flags).
- Gyms **scale** with your level-cap progression — early gyms won't stay trivial if you come back overleveled.

## Strategy tips

- **Match types.** Bring coverage for the gym's theme — fire gym? Bring Water or Rock.
- **Heal between battles.** Pokémon Centers and Healing Machines reset your team fully.
- **Type matchup reminders** — in-game **Knowlogy** shows weaknesses on hover.
- **Status moves are strong** in gym fights; Gym Leaders have deeper movesets than wild spawns.

## Rematches

Most Gym Leaders allow rematches on a cooldown. Rematch fights often:
- Scale harder than the original
- Give better loot
- Sometimes unlock Mega Stones or held items
